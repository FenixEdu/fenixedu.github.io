Governance


Leadership of this project is determined by the institutions who choose to actively contribute. The steering committee is composed of a group of dedicated individuals nominated by the contributing institutions to provide guidance and direction to the community. This committee is responsible for the project oversight and operates on a consensus based decision making taken in an open and transparent manner.

The current steering committee members are:

  • Ana Rute (Reitoria da Universidade de Lisboa)
  • Hugo Querido (QubIT)
  • Luis Cruz (Instituto Superior Técnico)
  • Pedro Rosa (Faculdade de Ciências)

FenixEdu is an open-source academic information platform.
