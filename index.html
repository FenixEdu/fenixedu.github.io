what is fenixedu?


FenixEdu is a modular software platform for academic and administrative management of higher education institutions. It provides an integrated solution that spans all levels of the academic management process: from high level management tasks to the daily communication between students, faculty and staff. Among other tools the platform also provides support for many administrative processes, such as procurement, resource management and quality assurance. The distributed nature of the platform enables effective and ubiquitous communication between all the stakeholders.

The FenixEdu platform provides a REST API, with strong authentication based on OAuth 2.0, which enables easy interoperability with existing systems, such as Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) and Content Management Systems (CMS), and is a convenient foundation for the development of a rich diversity of external applications. FenixEdu is integrated with an advanced identity management subsystem, which provides single sign-on, federation support, strong authentication based on the national e-ID card and support for European e-ID interoperability.

FenixEdu is developed as an open-source project, adopting the Lesser General Public Licence (LGPL), thus offering a basis for open collaboration, free distribution, open availability and customization capabilities to meet the requirements and needs of different institutions.

our users

students

Students are the driving force and the core purpose of any higher education institution, therefore addressing their needs is the cornerstone of FenixEdu. From admissions to graduation, FenixEdu covers the complete student life cycle, providing them with an integrated view of their academic activities and processes.

Managers

From the school's executive board to oversight committees and department chairs, FenixEdu provides decision makers with dedicated management views, customized to meet their specific needs.


faculty

Instructors find in FenixEdu integrated, easy-to-use, course management tools, comprising content management (CMS), planning, scheduling, summary publication, management of bibliographic references, document storage and indexing, e-learning support and evaluation, student management and communication.

staff

The administrative staff is the backbone of any institution and handles its daily operation, which involves a large number of distinct tasks. FenixEdu provides tools for such tasks, including: degree design, planning and approval; full management of academic records; room, course and faculty scheduling and distribution; tuition and fee payment control; service quality control via on-line surveys; validation of European Credit Transfer and Accumulation System (ECTS); diploma and certificate processing.
