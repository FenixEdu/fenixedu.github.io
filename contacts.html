Contact Information


As with most community based projects, it may not be easy for people new to the project to find the information they need or to know where to direct questions. If you send us an e-mail to hello (at) fenixedu.org we'll try to answer your questions as best we can, or redirect you to a more adequate channel.

Before contacting us please take into consideration that this project is not responsible for the maintnence of specific instalations of our software at any particular institution. If you need to contact someone because of a issue regarding a specific instalation please direct your contact to the local resources responsible for that instalation. The list below might help you find a local contact at your institution, but because this is an open source project, the list may not be complete.
