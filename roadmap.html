Roadmap


The FenixEdu organization hosts a number of products and modules. Even though some have a strong cohesion, or integrate well with one another, each individual project has its own development cycle and in most cases its own development team. The result is that we do not have one single roadmap for the entire organization, but rather multiple roadmaps, one for each software project hosted under our organization.

While we will list here the major upcomming releases for our most significant software projects, you should always check the specific detailed roadmap of each individual software project to get the complete planning. Here you can find a list of our current software projects under development.
